📱 iPhone Mail App

New Categorized Inbox:

  • Open the Mail app
  • Find our email

  • Select “Categorize Sender” → “Primary”

  • Go to Junk

  • Confirm when prompted

💻 Gmail (Desktop)

  • Click “Yes” to apply to future emails

Mark as Important:

  • Open the email → Click the flag icon or three dots → “Mark as important”
